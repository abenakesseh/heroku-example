About Us


House of Jollof is a Ghanaian-based restaurant that cooks and serves a variety of dishes and meals


We serve dishes from African countries aside Ghana, such as Nigeria and Kenya. ALl our meals are vegetarian and vegan based. None of the meals, except the Goat Soup are not cooked with any meat stock. All the dishes are flavorful and tasty and will have you wanting more after your first bite. Our most popular dishes are Jollof Rice, Fried Plantains, Goat Soup with Fufu and Egusi with Fufu.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to provide our customers with quality food and the best services ever which will have them coming back for more food. Also to create an environment where absolute satisfaction is our highest priority.


VISION: Through a shared commitment to excellence, we are dedicated to the uncompromising quality of food, service, people and profit, while taking exceptional care of our customers and staff.
